# Adam Corelli – Personal Site

Welcome to the personal website of **Adam Corelli**. This site features a curated collection of articles, photography, and a contact form for direct communication.

## About

This website is the digital home of Adam Corelli, where you’ll find:

- **Articles:** Thoughtful essays and posts on a range of topics that inspire, challenge, and inform.
- **Photography:** A showcase of original photography spanning landscapes, portraits, travel, and artistic imagery.
- **Contact:** An easy-to-use contact form to get in touch with Adam for collaboration, inquiries, or just to say hello.

## Features

- Clean, modern, and responsive design
- Well-organized navigation for articles and galleries
- Fast-loading image galleries
- Accessible, semantic HTML
- Integrated contact form (configurable for your preferred email provider)
- SEO-friendly metadata

## Getting Started

1. **Clone or download this repository.**
2. **Edit the content** in the respective directories (e.g. `/articles`, `/photos`, `/about.md`) as needed.
3. **Set up the contact form** by configuring the backend or email service (e.g. Formspree, Netlify Forms, or your own server).
4. **Deploy** the site to your preferred hosting provider (Netlify, Vercel, GitHub Pages, etc.).
5. **Configure your domain** (e.g. adamcorelli.com) to point to the deployed site.

## Customization

- Update site details, social links, and biography in the `/about.md` file.
- Add new articles in the `/articles` directory, using markdown or HTML.
- Upload new photographs to the `/photos` or `/gallery` directory.
- Adjust the styling and layout in the `/css` or `/styles` directory.
- Update the contact form endpoint in `contact.html` or the relevant component.

## Contact

You can reach Adam directly using the contact form on the website, or via social media links provided in the site footer.
